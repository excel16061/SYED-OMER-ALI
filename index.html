About Me

I am a software engineer and graduate student specializing in offline and resource-aware AI systems that can run reliably on constrained devices. My journey began in Hyderabad with a Diploma and B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, and continued into my current M.S. in Computer Science at Avila University, USA.

Across academic and industry work, a single question has guided me: How can we design intelligent systems that remain fast, trustworthy, and useful even when connectivity is weak or hardware is limited? This has led me to focus on offline and on-device machine learning, database and query optimization, and performance-conscious system design.

I am actively preparing for PhD studies in Computer Science, with long-term goals of building efficient, self-reliant AI systems for real-world environments and mentoring the next generation of researchers.

Research Interests

  • Resource-efficient deep learning, model compression, and latency-aware optimization
  • Database and query optimization for ML-driven systems
  • Secure and reliable systems for mobile and IoT environments
